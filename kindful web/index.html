OUR MISSION

The mission doing this project will help to reduce food wastage happening around the world. To avoid that we came up with a solution to donate leftover edible food for elders' homes and children's homes. As well as there is a donation part going on another side with the hope of providing the daily essentials for people who need them. By developing these applications, we are planning to create a connection between donors and related organizations. We hope our kindful donation system will be helpful when contacting people who need help and people who are willing to help. In this project, all the work processes are planned according to a time frame with the consideration of workload. Apart from that using this system we hope to provide a solution for food wastage in current society.

OUR VISION

According to a recently completed report, they have found out that half of food which is edible is wasted per meal in every restaurant in the world. this food crisis became a massive issue at the moment. Although society is talking about this matter, there are no practical solutions has been proposed in the world to get rid of this matter. And half of the people waste goods or clothes that are reusable in Sri Lanka. So, with the hope of solving these matters we have introduced a project idea called Kindful. This makes a smarter way of connecting donors and receivers. In here hotel managers act like food donors and receivers are the set of people who need help
