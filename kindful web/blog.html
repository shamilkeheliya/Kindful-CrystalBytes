Give Helping Hands

Now a days, wastage of food has become a major wastage in Sri Lanka among society and the hotels and functions too. And food manufacturers waste tons of food daily. Besides, People who are willing to donate foods and essential items like us need to personally visit the organization such as Elder Orphanages and Child Orphanages to donate food or another item or people who would like to donate should search on the websites to find locations and the details of the orphanages.

But this process is a difficult task to do daily and difficult to keep in track when it is needed. so kindfull help us a lot for this matter.
